# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and collaborative.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss technical tradeoffs in good faith
- provide actionable feedback
- assume mistakes are fixable
- keep communication specific and professional

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- dismissive or hostile language
- publishing private information without permission
- intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project spaces.
